Public Safety Notice Issued for Columbus Campus

Public Safety Notice

Campus Area, Off Campus Area, Medical Center, Academic Area, Residence Halls


Date:
2013-8-26

Alert Code:
2013-5

The following crime occured on campus:

On 08-26-13 at 7:17 pm, OSU Police received a report of an attempted robbery on Woody Hayes Drive near the Olentangy River bridge. The female student victim said that she was stopped by a white male suspect in his mid-twenties, accompanied by two other white males and a white female (all in their mid-twenties), in a red GMC sport utility vehicle traveling on Woody Hayes Drive. The suspect demanded her book bag while brandishing a red handgun. The suspect vehicle then left the area east bound after the victim refused to surrender property.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Anyone with information concerning this crime should contact either the University Police, 292-2121 or Columbus Police, 645-4545. You may also report information anonymously to the Central Ohio Crime Stoppers at 461-TIPS or the University Crime Stoppers Tips line at 247-TIPS.
